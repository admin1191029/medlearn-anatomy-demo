The Femur and Hip Joint

Duration 18 min · Human Anatomy I - Musculoskeletal

This lesson covers the anatomy of the femur, the head and neck of femur, and how it articulates with the acetabulum to form the hip joint. We also review the main ligaments that stabilize the joint.
